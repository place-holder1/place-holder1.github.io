About Elijah Gill

I am a very, very creative and explorative person.

When I a very young child, I was first exposed to the world of web-based games with a wide variety of genres. From playing single-player games like the Papa's series to online-web based games created by Goodgame Studios, these early experiences fueled the start of an era for my curiosity. Years later, I managed to pick up the Wii, and I had an extravagent time playing party-based games and being immersed with my favorites Wii Sports Resort and PokePark 2: Wonders Beyond. At the age of 13, I acquired the Xbox One to play Minecraft, and I had spent years crafting and designing worlds and puzzles for other players to join and test out.

I play a wide variety of genres. Some of them are, but not limited to:

    Tycoon Management
    Strategy
    Simulation
    Sports
    Role Playing
    Sandbox
    Puzzles
