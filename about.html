About Elijah Gill

I am a very, very creative and explorative person.

As a young child, I delved into the world of web-based games on PC, exploring diverse genres from single-player adventures like the Papa's series to immersive online games by Goodgame Studios. This early fascination sparked a lifelong curiosity. Later, I enjoyed memorable experiences on the Wii, indulging in lively games such as Wii Sports Resort and PokePark 2: Wonders Beyond. At 13, I acquired an Xbox One, where I dedicated myself to crafting intricate worlds and puzzles in Minecraft, inviting others to join and explore. Just a few years ago, I moved back to PC, but instead I delved into franchise games such as Metal Gear Solid V and Epic Battle Fantasy 5.


I play a wide variety of genres. Some of them are, but not limited to:

    Tycoon Management
    Strategy
    Simulation
    Sports
    Role Playing
    Sandbox
    Puzzles
